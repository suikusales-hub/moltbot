Data Privacy Analysis

THE PRIVACY FRONTIER

Agentic AI crosses a "Privacy Rubicon" by moving from observation to action. When your assistant remembers everything, security becomes a question of containment.

01 The "Always-On" Vulnerability

Unlike a chat session that closes when you close the tab, Moltbot is designed to be always on. It lives in your terminal, watches your file system, and waits for triggers. This persistence creates a significantly larger attack surface.

> SYSTEM_STATUS: MONITORING
> ACCESS_LEVEL: FULL_DISK
> SESSION_DURATION: INFINITE
> WARNING: CONTINUOUS EXPOSURE DETECTED

The Memory Trap

"Persistent Memory" is a double-edged sword. While it makes the agent helpful, it also means that if malware breaches the agent, the data exposure is historical, not just momentary.

Action vs. Observation

Traditional social media algorithms observe us to serve ads. Agentic AI acts as us. This shift from passive profiling to active impersonation is the core privacy issue.
