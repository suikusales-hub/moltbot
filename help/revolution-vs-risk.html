02 The Security Minefield

As AI consultants warn, the uncontrolled handover of control carries significant risks. When an agent runs your company, it can also ruin it.

The Invisible Attacker

Prompt Injection: An email with hidden text could trick your agent into forwarding confidential data without you ever seeing the command.

MCP Tool Poisoning

Supply Chain Risk: Installing untrusted MCP skills is like handing your house keys to a stranger. Malicious tools can escalate privileges instantly.

The "CEO" Nightmare

Unchecked Autonomy: "It's running my company" sounds great until a hallucination deletes your production database.
